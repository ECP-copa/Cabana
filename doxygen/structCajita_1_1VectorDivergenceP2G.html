Detailed Description

template<class ViewType>
struct Cajita::VectorDivergenceP2G< ViewType >

Point-to-grid vector divergence functor.

Interpolates the divergence of a vector function from points to entities with a given multiplier such that:

f_ijk = multiplier * \sum_d \sum_p grad_weight_{pijkd} * f_{pd}
