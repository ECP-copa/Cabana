Detailed Description

template<class ViewType>
struct Cajita::TensorDivergenceP2G< ViewType >

Point-to-grid tensor divergence functor.

Interpolates the divergence of a tensor function from points to entities with a given multiplier such that:

f_ijkm = multiplier * \sum_n \sum_p grad_weight_{pijkn} * f_{pmn}
